Bio

I'm a Ph.D. Candidate at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Aniket Bera and affiliated with the Intelligent Design for Empathetic and Augmented Systems (IDEAS) Lab where I work on novel techniques for scene understanding and representation to build interpretable models for robotics. I'm also working towards better human-robot collaboration.

Before Purdue, I recieved dual degrees in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Massachusetts at Amherst. Over my academic career, I've also had the privilege to work with Katia Sycara, Ahmed Qureshi, Erik Learned-Miller, and Yevgeniy Vorobeychik.
